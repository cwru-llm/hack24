About

The LLM workshop will focus on the practical use and implementation of large language models. Participants will explore core topics such as training models on high-performance computing systems, the fundamentals of machine learning, and prompt engineering. The workshop will also include hands-on sessions, allowing students to work on tasks related to natural language processing and other real-world applications of LLMs. Attendees will gain insights into state-of-the-art techniques and tools used in the field, with guidance from researchers active in the area.
